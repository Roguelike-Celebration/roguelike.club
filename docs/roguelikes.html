What are roguelikes?

Roguelike games (Wikipedia) are intricate and fun computer games that have a history back to 1980 — and they've influenced many games along the way (including Diablo, Spelunky, and Dwarf Fortress). The most famous roguelike is probably Nethack, which looks like this:
