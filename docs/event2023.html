When and Where?

Roguelike Celebration 2023 will be held on Saturday and Sunday October 21st and 22nd, 2023 as a virtual event in our custom-built multiplayer game/chat space. Talks will also be streamed to Twitch and YouTube.

Our call for presenters is now open!

Technical talks, game design lessons, creative uses of procedural generation, interactive roguelike performances - we want to hear about it all! Submit a talk proposal here. The deadline for proposals is June 30, 2022.
