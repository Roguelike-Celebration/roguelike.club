When and Where?

Roguelike Celebration 2024 will be held on Saturday and Sunday October 19 - October 20, 2024 as a virtual event in our custom-built multiplayer game/chat space. Talks will also be streamed to Twitch and YouTube.

There will also be a free preview event September 8, 2023, 1pm Pacific in the same social space, with talks streamed to Twitch and YouTube:

  • James Cartlidge will be joining us in a fireside chat style discussion about the subjects of his upcoming book, The Rise of the Roguelite: Inside a Gaming Phenomenon!
  • Andrew Cedotal will be presenting a talk: "Literary Origins of the Rogue Archetype"!

Check back here for more details and a schedule as the event approaches.
